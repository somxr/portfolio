BIO

I am a digital creator and an aspiring designer in mixed reality (XR), which is an umbrella term for augmented + virtual reality (AR+VR). Based in Montreal, Canada, I am set to graduate from Concordia University's computer science program in May 2022. In the meantime, I develop augmented reality experiences in the form of Instagram filters using Spark AR I find social AR to be among the most versatile forms of self-expression as well as marketing tools in modern technology.
