BIO

I'm a digital creator and graphics/web developer. Currently, I'm a Computation Arts student at Concordia University. I'm passionate about interactive art and media. I create video games and Augmented Reality experiences because I see these mediums as powerful tools for self-expression. Previously, I graduated with a bachelor’s in computer science, which led to my role as Assistant Technical Director at renowned visual effects studio Framestore. I spent two years writing pipeline code to support 3D artists creating blockbuster movies.
